Sprint Communication Standards
One Consolidated Round Per Phase
Each phase has exactly one feedback round. All comments, changes, and questions must be compiled into a single message — not delivered piecemeal. Scattered feedback will be consolidated before actioning.
24-Hour Feedback Window
From the moment a phase presentation is delivered, the client has 24 hours to return consolidated feedback. Silence after 24 hours is treated as approval and the sprint advances. This is a contractual term — reference Section 6 of the signed agreement.
Locked Phases Are Final
Once a phase is approved, it is architecturally locked. Requesting changes to a locked phase triggers the scope-extension clause and invalidates the 14-day deadline. This protects both parties.
Direct Line for Operational Changes
Additional raw assets or urgent operational changes — contact directly via WhatsApp/Telegram: +251 92 549 3131. Do not send raw assets via email during the sprint.
Presentation Format
Each phase is presented as a structured Figma deck or PDF. Feedback must reference specific slides or elements — general impressions without specifics cannot be actioned within the sprint timeline.
Additional Business Card Contacts
All additional executive names, titles, phone numbers, and emails must be submitted by Day 9 (May 31, 7PM) at the latest. Late submissions cannot be guaranteed within the 14-day window.
